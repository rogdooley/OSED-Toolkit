WinDbg first-response (x86) checklist

1) Prove it’s your crash:
  !analyze -v
  r

2) Capture EIP + stack:
  r eip
  r esp
  dds esp L40

3) If you suspect your input is on stack:
  db esp L100
  da esp

4) Exception context:
  .exr -1
  .ecxr

5) Backtrace:
  k
  kv

6) Log it:
  .logopen /t crashlog.txt
  !analyze -v
  r
  k
  dds esp L40
  .logclose

Then run:
  python -m Tools.crashtriage.cli.triage_crash -l <pattern_len> --input crashlog.txt
